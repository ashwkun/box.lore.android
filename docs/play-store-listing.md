## Full description

```
boxlore is a free Android podcast app and podcast player that helps you find what to listen to next — not just scroll the same charts.

Subscribe to podcasts, stream or download episodes for offline listening, build a queue, and discover shows with semantic search and personalized For You picks. No ads. No paywall for search, recommendations, or downloads.

WHY BOXLORE?

• Podcast player built for Android with Material 3
• Subscribe to podcasts and never miss an episode (optional notifications)
• Search podcasts and episodes by meaning — not only exact title keywords
• Personalized For You picks from your listening, genres, and subscriptions
• Swipe curiosity cards to discover podcasts you would not have searched for
• Browse podcast charts and categories across US, India, UK, and France
• Home mixtape when you just want to press play and keep going

LISTEN YOUR WAY

• Stream podcasts or download episodes for offline listening
• Playback speed from 0.5x to 1.5x, sleep timer, queue, and skip controls
• Chapters and synced transcripts when a show provides them
• Video podcast playback where available
• Android Auto support for listening in the car

A BEAUTIFUL PLAYER

• Material 3 podcast app UI with gesture-friendly navigation
• Dynamic colors from podcast artwork in the full-screen player
• Dark themes, including AMOLED-friendly options
• Fast artwork loading so lists and the player stay responsive

YOUR LIBRARY, ORGANIZED

• Listening history and liked episodes
• Smart downloads and per-show auto-download — both optional
• Import and export podcast subscriptions with OPML
• Back up and restore your library with JSON

DAILY BRIEFING

• Optional region-based news brief with audio and a full script — use it when you want it, skip it when you do not

A NOTE ON DISCOVERY

Semantic search and podcast recommendations use a daily-updated index of popular chart podcasts that grows over time. You can still subscribe, search, stream, and download podcasts across the wider catalog like any podcast app.

Looking for a free podcast player with offline downloads, a smart queue, and ad-free podcast discovery on Android? Download boxlore today.

Feedback and ideas welcome via the in-app form or at support@aswin.cx
```
